DESIGN AND FABRICATION OF MINIMUM QUANTITY LUBRICATION

  • In Normal CNC machiens are using the coolent to reduces the heat , friction and surface roughness using this type of liquid coolent leads to usage of more coolent , wet floor and wet metal chips. to overcome this problem.
  • we introduce minimum quantity lubrication system. by using this mql system we can reduces the amount of coolent used , dry floor , dry metal chips as well.
  • The process of mql is the mixing the lubricant with nano particals and compressed air after mixing all these elements we driectly spraying in between the tool and workpiece can makes the metal cutting more efficient.
